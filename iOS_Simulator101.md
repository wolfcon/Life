## Screen Recording

### Use QuickTime Player

Just record mac Screen!

> Right click `QuickTime` app in Dock and `Record`.
>
> Stop in top menu bar right corner.

### Use Command Line Tool

#### Start

```shell
xcrun simctl io booted recordVideo videoName.mov
```

#### End

Just `ctrl` + `c`
